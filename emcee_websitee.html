a little about me

Fairs, expos, concerts, podcasts — I adapt to the room.

Looking to put a smile on your audience's faces? Then I'm the emcee for you. With experience hosting everything from government-organised community events and roadshows to professional conferences and radio shows, I've learned how to read a crowd fast and adjust on the fly.

Whether it's 100 industry professionals at a product launch or 50,000 people at Comic Con, my job is the same: keep things moving, keep it fun, and make sure your event is smooth-sailing from start to finish.
