Fairs, expos, concerts, podcasts — I adapt to the room.

Looking to put a smile on your audience's faces? Then I'm the emcee for you. With experience hosting everything from community carnivals and product launches to national broadcasts and radio shows, I've learned how to read a crowd fast and adjust on the fly.
